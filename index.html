About Us

Simple Approach

Wellness journey doesn’t have to be complicated. Our body has so much more potential than what we generally think. We provide workshops, classes, and retreats where you can experience your own power of your own mind and body through simple and natrual methods such as breathing exercises, meditation, and cold exposure.

Continuous Learning

Did you know that you can directly influence your own immune system without drugs or vaccines? We take the current health crisis seriously, and we believe by understanding our own physiology better, we can build a healthier community. Besides delivering profound experiences, we also teach the science behind the wellness practices.

We're a community

Sometimes it’s just so much easier with a community. No matter where you are at with your journey, you are welcome. Are you having an awakening moment in life and new to the wellness journey? We are so stoked for you! Are you already practicing or teaching? Come join us, let’s breathe and grow together!

Our Offerings

Here are various types of events we host.

Wim Hof Method Fundamentals Workshop

A half day workshop where you will learn all the fundmental techniques and information about Wim Hof Method that you can integrate in your life immediately. This is recommended for anyone who’s new to the method.

Weekly Classes

We provide weekly classes where you can drop in to experience the power of breathing exercises. It’s a great way to get in touch with the community and keep up with ongoing practices.

One on One Coaching

If you are looking for something more personal and customized for your specific needs, one-on-one sessions are available. Contact us for scheduling.

Corporation Wellness

If you are a business owner or a team lead looking for a way to ensure your employee’s well being, we can provide a customized session for your team. Experience a team building activity unlike any others.

Sungho Spark

Instructor

Sungho is a Korean American, a full-time software engineer, and a certified Wim Hof Method instructor.Working with computers days and nights, he was often getting sick. While he knew deep down that he had to change his lifestyle, he couldn’t break his habits of bad diets, computer games, and drinking.While searching for motivations he was introduced to the Wim Hof Method. The Vice documentary intrigued him so much he had to fly over to Poland to learn from Wim.Now two years later, he is practicing surfing, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and yoga on a daily basis.He claims that the Wim Hof Method has been the key component to shift his lifestyle, and he is excited to share his practice.
